I was born in Decatur, Georgia on Thursday October 7, 1999 at 9:45 PM.

My mother came up with my name when she heard about Olivia Newton John and decided that if she ever had a child she would name her Olivia, because she thought it was pretty.

My name was first used in this spelling by William Shakespeare in his play the “Twelfth Night” (1602).

There is a Latin word oliva meaning “olive” or “olive tree” that the name could have been based on.

I live in two homes that I spend time in depending on the week’s schedule. In my mom’s house, I live with my mother and her fiancé in Conyers. In my dad’s house, I live with my father in an apartment in north Atlanta.
